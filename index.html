About

Harry is a Documentary Producer / Director based in London. An experienced producer-director with a cinematic eye, he specialises in human-interest stories that get under the skin of their subjects.

Over the past seven years, Harry has crafted ambitious prime-time factual, specialist factual, and observational programmes for BBC One, BBC Two, Channel 4, Netflix, and Hulu.

His credits include the BAFTA-winning Ambulance, BAFTA-nominated Freddie Flintoff's Field of Dreams, and the critically acclaimed Educating Yorkshire (Series 2).

Alongside his directorial work, Harry operates a Sony FX6 on professional productions and builds digital tools that help documentary makers and journalists capture authentic human stories — including StoryCoPilot, his AI-assisted interview companion.
